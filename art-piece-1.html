Starry Night

by Vincent van Gogh

Year of Creation: 1889

Category: Abstract Landscape, Post-Impressionism

Description

Starry Night is an oil on canvas painting by post-impressionist artist Vincent van Gogh. Painted in June 1889, this iconic work depicts a swirling night sky over a small French village. The painting is characterized by its dynamic brushwork, bold colors, and emotional intensity. Van Gogh's unique style transforms the night sky into a turbulent, dreamlike landscape, making it one of the most recognizable paintings in the world.

Medium

Oil on canvas
