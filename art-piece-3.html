The Birth of Venus

by Sandro Botticelli

Year of Creation: 1484

Category: Renaissance, Mythological

Description

The Birth of Venus is a masterpiece of the Early Renaissance, painted by Sandro Botticelli around 1484. This iconic artwork depicts the goddess Venus emerging from the sea foam. The painting is celebrated for its graceful composition, delicate color palette, and mythological subject matter. Botticelli's work represents a pivotal moment in art history, blending classical mythology with Renaissance humanist ideals. The painting showcases the artist's masterful skill in depicting human beauty and natural elegance.

Medium

Tempera on canvas
